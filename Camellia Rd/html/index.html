About Us

Camellia Road, describes the path that we're taking with boba teas. Camellia, or Camellia sinensis, is the scientific name for the plant most teas come from. R&D are also the initials of the owners, Ricky & Darwin. We are inspired to bring a higher quality tea experience to San Diego while also creating a community.

Ingredients

“Great tea comes from great ingredients.”

Our drinks are made from brewed loose leaf teas that make the flavor of the tea stand out. Our house-made simple syrup is made from organic cane sugar. It is important for us to make our drinks with real milk and not creamer powder. Our milk teas are customizable, made-to-order, and adjustable. Get lactose-free milk or boba to make it extra sweet.
